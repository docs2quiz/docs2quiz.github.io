---
title: History
description: How past generation runs are saved and revisited.
---

Every completed generation run is saved automatically to your browser's local storage — there's no server-side history and nothing is synced between browsers or devices.

## What's saved

Each entry records the knowledge base, keywords and question counts used, and the full set of generated questions. Up to **50 entries** are kept; older ones are dropped once the cap is reached.

## Browsing history

The **History** page lists past runs newest-first, showing the knowledge base, timestamp, topic/question counts, and how many questions were successfully generated. Click an entry to see the full set of questions again, with the same display-language toggle and export menu available on the generate page.

## Clearing history

Click **Clear history** on the History page to wipe all saved entries from local storage. This only affects the current browser — it doesn't touch anything on the server.

:::note
Because history lives in local storage, it won't appear if you switch browsers, use a private/incognito window, or clear your browser data.
:::
